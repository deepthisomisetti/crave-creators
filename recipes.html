Paneer Butter Masala

Type: Vegetarian Curry

Flavor: Creamy, mildly spiced, rich

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • Salt, sugar to taste

Preparation:

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add tomato puree and cook for 5‚Äì7 mins.
  2. Add garam masala, kasuri methi, salt, sugar and simmer.
  3. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 mins.
  4. Stir in cream, simmer for 2 mins. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Gulab Jamun

Type: Indian Dessert

Flavor: Sweet, milky, syrupy

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Milk (as needed)
  • 2 cups sugar + 1.5 cups water
  • Cardamom pods, rose water (optional)

Preparation:

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, ghee. Add milk to form soft dough.
  2. Make smooth balls. Fry on low heat till golden.
  3. Boil sugar with water and cardamom. Add rose water.
  4. Soak fried balls in warm syrup. Let sit 1 hr before serving.

Banana Oat Smoothie

Type: Smoothie

Flavor: Creamy, filling, energizing

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)

Preparation:

  1. Blend oats first for a few seconds to make them finer.
  2. Add banana, milk, yogurt, and optional ingredients.
  3. Blend until smooth. Chill and serve!
